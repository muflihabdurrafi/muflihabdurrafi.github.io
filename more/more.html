This Page is More About Me!

My name is Muhammad Muflih Abdurrafi. I was born in Harapan Bunda Hospital, East Jakarta, Indonesia at Monday, 20th of May 2002. I am the fourth and last child of my parents. Throughout my childhood I went back and forth to Saudi Arabia because of my father's work.

I spent approximately six years in Saudi Arabia. Whilst living in there, I went to international schools since the first grade (year one) where I was able to learn and use English on the daily.

The other half of my childhood was spent in my home country, Indonesia. The first time going back to school in Indonesia was quite nerve-racking because I didn't understand how anything works in Indonesia. Thankfully I've always been able to speak my mother tongue, so the adaptation process was not as hard as it would be if I weren't able to.

At the end, I also spent six years in Indonesia. Two years of elementary school, one year of middle school, and three years of high school. I just realized that I approximately spent the same amount of time in Saudi and Indonesia during my school years, but the later years are the ones that really had more impact in my personal development.

After that I still continued my education in Indonesia. Currently studying Informatics in Jenderal Soedirman University.
